Bloxd Texture Pack Steps

Step 1

Go to Settings > Texture Pack > Custom

Step 2

Image Uploader

https://imgur.com/upload

Skyboxes

A skybox can be either Earth, or Custom
Earth example "type": "earth"
"inclination": "number"
"turbidity": "number"
"infiniteDistance": "number"
"luminance": "number"
"yCameraOffset": "number"
"azimuth":

Custom Example: "type": "custom",
"nx": ""url"",
"ny": "url",
"nz": "url",
"px": "url",
"py": "url",
"pz": "url",
