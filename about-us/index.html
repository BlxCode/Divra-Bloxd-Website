About Us





Our mission is to customize Bloxd.Io and to make it accessable, free and easy to use.


Background

Divra Bloxd Extention's roots can be traced all the way back to the 31st of October, 2023 when BloxdMaster, a skilled programmer that also makes videos about bloxd.io tries to make a userscript for bloxd.io. There were a toltal of 500 installs on greasyfork; a massive success!

The Loss

On Feburary 12 2024, bloxd.io got hacked, and the reason was traced down to a hacked userscript, so the Arthur, the owner of bloxd banned all userscripts, and extentions including mines.

The comeback

2 Months later, Bloxd.io userscript programmer Bluify, pleaded Arthur to allow Chrome extentions on the webstore verified by Arthur, so I asked Arthur permission and He allowed it, so that's when Divra Bloxd Extention came in. Divra Bloxd Extention was basically Divra Bloxd Userscript, but with additional features.
